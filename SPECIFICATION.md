# System Specification: Healthy Habit Tracker

## 1. Introduction

### 1.1 Project Title
**Healthy Habit Planner** - Your Personal Healthy Habit, Nutrition & Workout Companion

### 1.2 Domain
**Health & Fitness Technology** (HealthTech/FitnessTech)

### 1.3 Domain Description
The system operates within the health and wellness domain, focusing on digital tools that assist individuals in managing their personal fitness and nutrition goals.

Many people struggle with maintaining consistent workout routines and tracking nutritional intake due to the lack of centralized and easy-to-use tools. FitPlan provides a structured platform where users can manage their workout schedules, record meals, and monitor progress toward their health goals.

### 1.4 Problem Statement
Many individuals attempting to maintain a healthy lifestyle rely on multiple disconnected tools such as notebooks, spreadsheets, or mobile apps to track workouts and nutrition. This fragmentation often results in inconsistent tracking, difficulty monitoring progress, and reduced motivation.

The purpose of the Healthy Habit PlanneR system is to provide a single integrated platform that enables users to plan workouts, log meals, track calorie intake, and monitor their overall fitness progress.

### 1.5 Individual Scope & Feasibility Justification

Healthy Habit Planner will be implemented as a web-based application consisting of a user interface, backend application services, and a database system.

Core features will include:

- User registration and authentication
- Workout plan management
- Meal and nutrition logging
- Daily calorie tracking
- Progress visualization through charts
- Goal tracking

The system will be designed with a modular architecture so that additional features can be added later if required. The initial implementation focuses on essential features that are feasible for a single developer to complete within the semester timeframe.
