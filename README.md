# 🏋️‍♂️ Health Habit Tracker - Personal Nutrition & Workout Planner

## 📋 Overview
Health Habit Tracker is a web-based health and fitness planning system designed to help individuals manage their workouts, track nutritional intake, and monitor progress toward their fitness goals.

The system provides users with the ability to create personalized workout plans, log meals, track calories, and monitor health progress over time.

FitPlan aims to simplify fitness management by providing a centralized platform that integrates nutrition tracking, workout planning, and progress analytics.

## 🎯 Purpose
In today's busy world, maintaining a healthy lifestyle requires consistent tracking and planning. NutriMove Planner solves this by providing an integrated platform where users can:
- Log daily meals and track nutritional intake (calories, macros, micros)
- Plan and record workout sessions with exercise libraries
- Monitor progress toward fitness goals (weight loss, muscle gain, maintenance)
- Receive personalized meal and workout recommendations based on their profile

## 👥 Target Users
- Fitness enthusiasts wanting to track their progress
- People on specific diets (keto, vegetarian, paleo, etc.)
- Beginners starting their fitness journey
- Athletes monitoring performance nutrition
